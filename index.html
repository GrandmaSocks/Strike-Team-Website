A Unique Garry's Mod Experience

A Gamemode mod for the 2006 game Garry's Mod with an emphasis on tactical, thought out player vs player combat. When playing on the Strike Team you have to get ready to infiltrate a enemy position and retrieve hostages. As a Terrorist you set down barricades and devise a plan to help you eliminate the Strike Team and keep your out of Strike Team's reach hostages.
